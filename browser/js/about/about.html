About

My name is Beckylee Dell. I'm a software engineer and a native New Yorker.

I graduated from Tufts University with a BA in Psychology, and began working at a nonprofit. While there, I started doing minor fixes to their website, and eventually became a full time developer. At that point, I was mostly self-taught, and had a special appreciation for CSS animations. I decided to follow my newfound passion for coding and, in the summer of 2015, I attended Fullstack Academy of Code to formally learn software engineering.

I am a fullstack engineer, with equal appreciation for the intricacies of UI, as well as the details of servers and databases. My goal is to never stop learning and to become a better developer every day.
